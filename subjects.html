Our Academic Curriculum

At Shree Sharada High School, we offer a holistic curriculum that supports intellectual, physical, and moral development. Our subjects are aligned with the Karnataka State Board and are enriched with co-curricular opportunities to shape well-rounded individuals.

Subjects Offered

Languages: Kannada, English, Hindi
Core Subjects: Mathematics, Science, Social Studies
Skill-Based: Computer Education, Craft
Physical & Mental Development: Physical Education, Yoga

Languages

Develop communication and linguistic skills in Kannada, English, and Hindi.

Mathematics

Focus on arithmetic, algebra, geometry, and problem-solving skills.

Science

Hands-on learning through experiments and concept-based understanding.

Social Studies

Understanding society, history, geography, and civic duties.

Computer Education

Basic digital literacy, typing, and educational software usage.

Physical Education

Yoga, exercises, and sports activities for a healthy lifestyle.

Focus by Grade

8th Standard

Focus on concept building and exam readiness. Students are trained for competitive exams like NMMS, Ramayana, and Mahabharatha, with many achieving state and national scholarships.

9th Standard

Emphasis on subject depth and holistic learning. Students engage in sports, cultural activities, and social work through clubs and community projects.

10th Standard

Targeted preparation for SSLC and NTSE exams. Special classes and remedial support ensure academic success. Career guidance programs prepare students for future choices.

Our Teaching Approach

We use smart classrooms, group activities, project-based learning, and regular assessments to make learning engaging and effective. Our faculty uses both traditional and modern techniques to ensure subject mastery.

Competitive Exam Support

Dedicated coaching is provided for competitive exams like NMMS and NTSE. Regular mock tests, previous paper solving, and focused mentoring are part of the strategy.

Beyond the Books

Co-curricular subjects such as Art & Craft, Yoga, and Physical Training help develop creativity, health, and life skills, ensuring students grow into confident individuals.
