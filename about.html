About Us

Providing Quality Education and Empowerment Since 1982

Welcome to Shree Sharada Highschool, Sullia

Shree Sharada Highschool was established in the year 1982 with the noble intention of educating young childrens from rural areas. The school is managed by Dakshina Kannada Gowda Vidya Sangha and has been a symbol of excellence in education for over a century. We strive to provide a safe, inclusive, and empowering environment where every student is given the opportunity to grow academically and socially.

Our Vision

To empower young children through holistic education by nurturing intellectual curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking—while instilling strong moral values, discipline, and compassion to help them become confident individuals and responsible citizens.

Our Mission

To provide an inclusive and supportive learning environment that fosters intellectual growth, creative expression, physical development, and ethical conduct—empowering students to become confident, compassionate, and capable individuals.

National Cadet Corps (NCC)

Our NCC unit fosters discipline, leadership, and patriotism among students. We actively participate in national-level camps, parades, and community service programs, helping students build confidence, responsibility, and team spirit.
