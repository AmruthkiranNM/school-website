Our School Blog

Annual Sports Meet 2025

The Annual Sports Meet 2025 was a grand success. Students actively participated in a variety of events including athletics, team games, and traditional Indian games. Our chief guest, a local Olympian, inspired the students with a powerful speech on discipline and dedication.


NSS Camp Highlights

Our students participated in a 7-day National Service Scheme (NSS) camp organized at a nearby rural village. They engaged in community service, cleanliness drives, tree plantation, and awareness campaigns on hygiene, education, and social responsibilities. The camp was a great opportunity to learn leadership, empathy, and teamwork.


Art & Cultural Fest

The cultural fest showcased various art forms including dance, singing, painting, and drama. The theme of this year's event was "Unity in Diversity", and each class performed unique skits representing different cultures of India.

The main visions of Shree Sharada Highschool is to enhance access to higher education, ensuring equity, especially for the vulnerable sections of society. We strive to improve the employability of students graduating from our institution and promote quality education. Vocational and skill-based education are key pillars, along with policies to strengthen research, innovation, and collaboration between institutions, industries, and society.

Our mission is to evolve a cost-effective and inclusive education system nationwide, without discrimination. We aim to transform school education by improving teaching methods and tracking measurable improvements in learning. We implement scalable programs that generate impactful data, guiding education policy and helping children from rural and urban areas reach the standards of elite schools.

Our school facilities are designed to adapt to evolving educational methods. We provide a safe, secure, and comfortable learning environment that is well-lit, ventilated, and aesthetically pleasing. Beyond infrastructure, our school includes high-quality systems for power, security, IT, and learning resources. The campus features playgrounds, outdoor learning areas, and safe access for transport and parking.
