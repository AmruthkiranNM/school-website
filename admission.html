Admission Process

Admissions are open for 8th, 9th, and 10th standards. We welcome all eligible students to apply.

Eligibility

  • Students must have passed the previous class (7th/8th/9th) from a recognized school.
  • Preference given to students from rural and economically weaker sections.
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) from previous school is mandatory.

How to Apply

  1. Collect the admission form from the school office or download from website (if available).
  2. Fill in the details and attach required documents.
  3. Submit the form to the school office between 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM on working days.
  4. Attend orientation and interview session (if required).
