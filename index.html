Our School Campus

Subjects Offered

Kannada English Hindi Mathematics Science SocialStudies ComputerEducation Craft PhysicalEducation along with Yoga

8th STANDARD

The class strength ranges from a minimum of 25 to a maximum of 50 students. We prepare our students for competitive exams such as NMMS, Ramayana, and Mahabharata. Many of them have qualified at the state and national levels, earning prestigious scholarships.

9th STANDARD

The class accommodates between 25 and 50 students. Alongside academics, we encourage students to actively participate in sports and cultural activities. Through various school clubs, they also engage in social service programs. Many have represented our school in guide conferences and community events.

10th STANDARD

The class has a strength ranging from 25 to 50 students. We provide focused coaching for NTSE and conduct special classes to support students in their SSLC exams. Those needing extra academic help are given encouragement and support to improve their performance. We also organize career guidance sessions to help students plan their future paths.

Our Facilities

Our school provides various facilities to the students.Because our school is government-aided,we provide free mid-day meal,textBooks,Milk,IronTablets,Egg/Banana etc.

Good Library

There is a well built library with good quality books.Students are free to access all the facilities of the library in the school.Also they can borrow books for their assignments and projects.

Computer Lab

Our school has a well-equipped computer lab with internet access. Students are introduced to basic computer skills, typing, and using educational software to enhance digital literacy.

Smart Classrooms

We use modern teaching aids like projectors and digital content to make learning more interactive and effective. Audio-visual teaching methods help students grasp difficult concepts easily.

Regular Exercises

Our school provides Physical Education to students.Regular Exercises along with Yoga are held to increase their concentration.Our physical education teachers also give well training to the students to participate in various levels of sports.

Tailoring

Our tailoring facility equips students with practical skills in stitching and garment design. It helps enhance creativity, handcrafting abilities, and opens doors to self-employment opportunities.

Tasty And Healthy Food

Mid-day meal facilities are provided to all the students.We also give special importance to the quality of food cooked and cleanliness.Milk and Iron tablets from the government are supplied to all the students on time.

Laboratory

Our well-equipped science laboratory allows students to perform experiments and explore scientific concepts hands-on. It promotes curiosity, observation skills, and practical understanding of theories.
